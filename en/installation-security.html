FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are the most common types of vandalism on charging stations?

The most common incidents are charging cable theft (copper), touchscreen damage, graffiti on chargers and attempts to hack payment systems. We also see vehicle impacts on poorly protected equipment.

How does connected CCTV work?

Our high-definition IP cameras with night vision are connected to our 24/7 monitoring center. When an intrusion is detected (AI analysis), an alert is triggered in real time and our teams can intervene remotely or dispatch a security agent on site.

What is the return on investment for security measures?

Our clients see a significant reduction in incidents after installing our solutions. Security costs are typically recouped within 6 to 12 months through lower repair costs, reduced equipment replacement and fewer operational losses.

Do you offer a free security audit?

Yes, we offer a free, no-obligation vulnerability audit to assess your infrastructure risks. Our experts inspect your sites, identify weaknesses and provide a detailed protection plan with pricing.
