Growth

A real career path

At JCSM, every technician can progress. Here are the steps you can take within the company.

1

IRVE Technician

Installation and maintenance of AC/DC charging stations. Manufacturer training from the start.

2

Senior Technician

Complex interventions, advanced diagnostics, commissioning of high-power DC. Technical reference in the field.

3

Team Lead

Managing a team of technicians. Intervention planning, quality monitoring and reporting.

4

Regional Manager

Running a geographic area. Team management, direct client relationships and performance objectives.
