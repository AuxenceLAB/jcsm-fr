Installation

Installation assignments too

Beyond maintenance, JCSM also entrusts charging station installation work to its qualified partners.

Private AC

Apartment buildings, single-family homes, businesses. Wall-mounted and pedestal chargers, single- and three-phase.

Public AC

Public car parks, shopping centres, hotels. Chargers with OCPP supervision and integrated payment.

Private DC

Corporate fleets, bus depots, logistics centres. Fast chargers from 50 to 150 kW.

Public DC

Rapid charging stations, motorway service areas. High-power chargers from 150 to 400 kW, CCS and CHAdeMO.
