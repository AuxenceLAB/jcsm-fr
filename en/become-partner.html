Required profile

What we expect from our technical partners.

Electrical qualifications

B2V, BR, BC minimum. High-voltage certification (H1/H2) preferred for DC installations and HV/LV transformers.

IRVE experience preferred

Familiarity with AC chargers (EVBox, Schneider, Hager) and/or DC chargers (Kempower, Alpitronic, ABB). Training available for certain brands.

Regional or nationwide mobility

Ability to cover your geographic area. Own utility vehicle and tools preferred.

Independent or company status

Sole trader, LLC, or corporation -- any legal structure accepted. Professional liability insurance required.

Rigour and reporting

Intervention reports, before/after photos, strict adherence to safety protocols. Use of digital tools for tracking and follow-up.

Responsiveness

Ability to respond within 24 to 48 hours for corrective maintenance. Availability for occasional on-call duties is a plus.

What we offer

A structured partnership, not just one-off assignments.

Steady volume of interventions

Scheduled preventive and corrective maintenance in your area. No downtime -- a consistent flow of assignments.

JCSM technical support

Access to our technical team for remote diagnostics, manufacturer documentation, and escalation when needed.

Fast and transparent payment

Clear rate card by intervention type. Monthly invoicing, payment within 30 days.

Training on specific brands

On-site training available for the charger brands we deploy most (Kempower, ABB, EVBox).
