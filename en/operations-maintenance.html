Our team is fully trained on both OCPP 1.6 and the latest OCPP 2.0.1 protocol. We configure, troubleshoot and optimise charger-to-backend communication for any CSMS platform -- ensuring seamless transactions, smart charging profiles, and ISO 15118 Plug & Charge readiness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of chargers do you maintain?

We maintain all AC chargers (3.7 to 22 kW) and DC chargers (50 to 350 kW) from leading brands: ABB, Schneider, EVBox, Alfen, Kempower, Tritium and more. We also service the associated HV/LV transformers.

What is your response time?

Our contractual commitment is 4 hours in urban areas and 8 hours in rural areas for critical interventions. Our 24/7 support enables immediate remote diagnostics, resolving 40% of faults without an on-site visit.

Do you offer preventive maintenance?

Yes, our contracts include scheduled preventive visits (quarterly or biannual depending on the contract). We inspect connectors, electrical supply, firmware updates and equipment cleaning.

Do you operate in Belgium?

Yes, we cover mainland France and Belgium (Brussels, Wallonia, Flanders). Our teams are deployed across the territory to ensure fast response times.
