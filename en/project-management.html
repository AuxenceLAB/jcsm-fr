Frequently asked questions

What is IRVE project consulting (AMO)?

IRVE project consulting (AMO - Assistance to Project Owner) involves supporting the project owner through all phases of their charging station deployment: feasibility study, specification drafting, contractor selection, construction supervision and installation acceptance.

How much does an IRVE deployment project cost?

The cost varies based on the number of charging points, power output (AC or DC), civil engineering works and grid connection. We provide a detailed budget study from the scoping phase, identifying public subsidies (ADVENIR, CEE) to optimize your investment.

What is the typical duration of an IRVE project?

A complete project (study + installation + commissioning) typically takes 3 to 6 months. Simple projects (a few AC stations) can be completed in 4 to 8 weeks. Large-scale multi-site deployments require 6 to 12 months of management.

Do you work with CPOs and EMSPs?

Yes, we collaborate with Charge Point Operators (CPO) and e-Mobility Service Providers (EMSP) for the deployment, maintenance and supervision of their networks. Our turnkey approach covers France and Belgium.
